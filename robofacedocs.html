04

Hidden Holds

Hold actions are time-based. The spot under your finger does not matter once the hold begins.

Hold timeResult
Tap and releaseSelected expression shows immediately.
About 2.5 secondsExpression labels toggle on or off.
About 5 secondsEmotional Rescue QR/credit screen opens.
About 10 seconds totalBattery status screen opens.
05

Poke Patterns

Vidiotbox remembers recent taps. Rapid means about 2.5 seconds apart. Repeat means the same expression within about 6 seconds.

PatternReaction
HAPPY, BEAT-BOP, WACKY, or SURPRISEDQueues PLAYFUL.
ANGRYShows ANGRY and queues ANNOYED.
DREAMING twiceQueues DREAMY.
SLEEPING twice while calmQueues DREAMY.
PLAYFUL/ANNOYED alternation twiceQueues TORN.

Queued moods wait until the post-touch hold ends and Vidiotbox returns to idle.

Mood Parade

MoodTriggerSequence
CALMDefault idleNEUTRAL -> PROUD -> HAPPY -> NEUTRAL

07

Listening / VU

The microphone level is compared to the recent background level. Vidiotbox chooses the highest matching sound reaction.

Green = background noise floor.
Blue = LOVING floor.
Yellow = GIF/image trigger.
Red = ANNOYED threshold. Very loud sounds win.
SoundResult
QuietBackground adjusts.
SoftCURIOUS can start.
Sustained friendlyLOVING can show after about 0.5 seconds in range.
StrongGIF/image plays after the decision window.
Very loudANNOYED starts and GIF/image playback is skipped.

09

Battery / Rest

Vidiotbox checks battery voltage after the startup grace period, then sleeps before the LiPo gets dangerously low.

StateApproximate value
Fullraw about 2445, about 4.2 V
Resumeraw 2100, about 3.4 V
Low warningraw 2000, about 3.2 V
Flat referenceraw about 1560, about 2.7 V

Three consecutive low readings trigger the Charge Me warning and deep sleep. Charge-rest checks again every 15 minutes.

10

Personality

The personality file lives at /faceimator/personality.txt. It reflects recent behavior, then fades back toward neutral.

AxisLow sideHigh side
EnergyCalmer, sleepierMore playful
PatienceEasier to annoyMore tolerant

To reset personality, open the setup portal, go to /personality, and use the reset button.
